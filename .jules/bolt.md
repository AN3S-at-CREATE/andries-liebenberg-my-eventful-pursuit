## 2025-04-05 - Optimize Canvas FillStyle Batching & Hooks
**Learning:** In highly dense HTML5 canvas animations (like a starfield with 24k elements), grouping objects by their `fillStyle` and rendering them in batches drastically reduces the overhead of string parsing and context switching within the tight requestAnimationFrame loop. Also, when early-returning a component on mobile to prevent DOM rendering overhead (e.g. `if (isMobile) return null;`), all Framer Motion `useTransform` hooks must still be explicitly declared unconditionally at the top of the component to obey React Rules of Hooks.
**Action:** Before optimizing loop logic, ensure arrays or objects are pre-sorted or grouped by their most expensive state changes (like colors or gradients) during initialization. Always hoist hooks above early returns.
